These Referral Partner Program Terms & Conditions ("Agreement") govern your participation in the Riggins Strategic Solutions Professional Partner Network. By registering as a Partner, referring clients, or accepting any benefit from Riggins Strategic Solutions ("Company"), you agree to be bound by these terms.

## 1. Program Structure & Classification

The Partner Network consists of three distinct tiers. Your classification determines your eligibility for compensation and your ethical obligations.

### Tier 1: Strategic Partner (No-Fee / Ethical Compliance)

- **Eligibility:** Attorneys (Estate, Elder Law, Probate), CPAs, Trust Officers, and other fiduciaries prohibited from accepting referral fees.
- **Compensation:** **ZERO ($0.00).** Strategic Partners are strictly prohibited from receiving any financial compensation, kickbacks, or "things of value" for referrals.
- **Benefit:** In lieu of compensation, Riggins Strategic Solutions provides a **Client Discount** (10-15%) directly to the referred client.
- **Ethical Compliance:** This structure is designed to comply with **NC State Bar Rule 5.4** (Professional Independence of a Lawyer) and **AICPA Code of Professional Conduct** regarding referral fees and independence.

### Tier 2: Affiliate Partner (Commission-Based)

- **Eligibility:** Senior Move Managers, Professional Organizers, Content Creators, and non-fiduciary professionals.
- **Compensation:** Affiliate Partners are eligible to receive a commission (percentage or flat fee) on the sale of digital products (e.g., Blueprint Core, Blueprint Premium) and specific consulting packages, subject to federal and state laws.
- **Independent Contractor Status:** You are an independent contractor, not an employee. You are responsible for all local, state, and federal taxes on your earnings. A Form 1099-NEC will be issued if earnings exceed $600 in a calendar year.

### Tier 3: Broker Partner (Real Estate Referral)

- **Eligibility:** Active, licensed Real Estate Brokers (NC or Nationwide).
- **Compensation:** Referral fees are paid strictly on a **Broker-to-Broker** basis in accordance with **NCREC Rules** and **RESPA**.
- **Requirement:** A separate, signed **Real Estate Referral Agreement** (e.g., NCAR Form 770) is required for each transaction. No referral fee will be paid without a valid, active license and a signed agreement.

## 2. RESPA & Anti-Kickback Compliance

**Strict Adherence to RESPA:** Riggins Strategic Solutions is a consulting firm, but its founder is a licensed Real Estate Broker. To ensure strict compliance with the **Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act (RESPA)**:

- **No Payment for Settlement Services:** No referral fee, commission, or thing of value will be paid to any person for the referral of business related to a federally related mortgage loan or real estate settlement service, *unless* that person is a licensed real estate broker and the payment is a compliant broker-to-broker referral fee.
- **Consulting vs. Brokerage:** "Affiliate Commissions" (Tier 2) are paid strictly for the sale of *consulting services* and *digital education products*. They are **never** tied to the closing of a real estate transaction or the sale of a property.

## 3. Non-Exclusivity & Independence

- **Non-Exclusivity:** Participation in this program is non-exclusive. You are free to refer clients to other service providers.
- **No Agency:** This Agreement does not create a partnership, joint venture, agency, or employment relationship. You have no authority to make representations or bind Riggins Strategic Solutions to any contract.

## 4. Confidentiality & Data Privacy

- **Client Data:** You agree to obtain necessary client consent before sharing their contact information with Riggins Strategic Solutions.
- **HIPAA:** If you are a covered entity under HIPAA, you agree not to share Protected Health Information (PHI) without a valid Business Associate Agreement (BAA) or client authorization.

## 5. Termination

Riggins Strategic Solutions reserves the right to terminate your participation in the Partner Program at any time, with or without cause. Upon termination:

- **Tier 2:** Any unpaid, valid commissions earned prior to termination will be paid.
- **Tier 3:** Pending real estate referrals with a signed agreement will be honored in accordance with that specific agreement.
